## Session: AI in Agile: How to Leverage AI for Smarter Sprints & Roadmaps

**1 May 2026 03:00 PM** | The Last Starfighter - Theatre 14 (12,13)  
*Track: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning*

AI isn’t just disrupting industries; it’s redefining how Agile team's work. From backlog grooming to sprint planning, modern tools like ChatGPT, Jira Assist, and GitHub Copilot are helping Agile leaders and teams move faster, think smarter, and focus more on value delivery than manual overhead.

In this forward-looking session, we’ll explore how AI is transforming the Agile lifecycle. You’ll see real-world examples and a live demo showing how AI can assist with generating user stories, refining acceptance criteria, summarizing retrospectives, and even forecasting roadmap scenarios.
