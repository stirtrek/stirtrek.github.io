What Is Stir Trek?
Stir Trek is normally a one-day conference focused on teaching software developers, and others in the industry, the latest and greatest in technologies, techniques, and tools. The full day of content is always concluded with a screening of a blockbuster film on its (originally scheduled) opening day. Pretty sweet, huh?

AMC Easton 30, Columbus, OH But this year we're also going to be a hybrid virtual event too! Details are still to come, but we can't wait to hang out with you again.
