## Session: Cutting Through the Hype: Building High Performing Teams

**1 May 2026 11:30 AM** | Interstellar - Theatre 4 (5,6,7,8,9)  
*Track: Professional Growth & Leadership*

Whether you’re a leader or an individual contributor, you rely on teams to get meaningful work done. We’ve been working in teams forever, yet some groups move fast and deliver consistently while others seem permanently stuck in the slow lane.

It’s not because one team has mythical “10x engineers” or the latest AI tools. Those things can amplify performance, but they don’t create it. High‑performing teams are built on a foundation of strong people practices and clear, healthy standards.

Designed for new leaders or anyone seeking a fresh perspective, this session breaks down a practical approach to building great teams by focusing on people and standards—and the way the two reinforce each other. By the end of this session, not only will you have ideas on how to improve your team, but also a concrete roadmap you can apply immediately.
