What Is Stir Trek?
Stir Trek is a one-day conference focused on teaching software developers, and others in the industry, the latest and greatest in technologies, techniques, and tools. The full day of content is always concluded with a screening of a blockbuster film on its (originally scheduled) opening day. (Editor's note: 2024 may be less predictable than normal re: film release dates.) Pretty sweet, huh?
