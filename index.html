Stir Trek is Friday May 7th!

We are less than 48 hours away from Stir Trek: 2021 Virtual Edition (We Miss You) and we wanted to remind you of a few items.

Livestream

This year's event will be livestreamed on the Stir Trek YouTube channel on May 7th, 2021 from 10am-4pm Eastern Time. The schedule for the event is at https://stirtrek.com/schedule

Slack

Please join our Slack to join in discussions, ask questions during sessions, and interact with other attendees from the Stir Trek community.

Stir Scholarship

Stir Scholarship is a scholarship for young women pursuing a degree in software engineering. The heart of Stir Scholarship is to promote gender diversity within the IT profession. Please consider making a donation: https://www.stirscholarship.org/

Store Trek

Since we aren't doing registration boxes this year, how do you get your swag / shirt and help the Stir Trek community keep going? Check out our online store with a curated selection of brand new items and previous Stir Trek shirts: https://store.stirtrek.com
